14 CONCLUSIONS ABOUT MOBILITY, MIGRATION & BORDERS

With AAAAARG.FAIL/ORG being sued, thousands of Indian scholars are not going to be able to obtain writings about theoretical and philosophical texts.

“To readers based in places like India, a collection with this breadth is simply unavailable and, on first sight, unimaginable, as these books often sell at more than three or four times the price of a bestselling novel.“
- Scroll.in

These scholars have to be able to produce critical research, but this becomes increasingly diffi- cult to make due to essential material that is/was available at AAAAARG.FAIL. As a reaction towards this lawsuit, this book has been produced in order to tackle the law and fight for the right to spread information. This book should not be taken seriously, nor does it really have a function outside of the message it is trying to spread. In order to bypass laws, only the conclusions from a selection of books about mobility, migration and borders have been taken. Also this way only the essential information from these text can be easily spread around. The A4 format is chosen so it can be easily printed at home, reproduced and carried further.
